Puerto Rican rice and beans

Description:

  • 1/4 cup vegetable/canola oil
  • 2 cups white rice long or medium grain
  • 1/2 cup sofrito
  • 4 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon adobo seasoning
  • 2 teaspoon sazon
  • 15.5 ounces pink/red kidney beans (or 1 cup cooked beans)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup Spanish green olives

Steps

  1. First, rinse the rice a few times and remove any dirt or foreign pieces. Rinse until water is no longer cloudy. Then, drain the water from the rice.
  2. Heat oil in a caldero or dutch oven over medium-high heat. Once oil is hot, add the rice. Toast and mix for 2-3 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together sofrito, tomato sauce, adobo, and sazon. Pour and mix into the rice.
  4. Combine beans and olives with the rice. Add 2 cups of water, about 1 inch above the rice. Mix.
  5. Without a lid, bring the rice to a boil over medium-high heat. Cook until the liquid has been mostly cooked off. Then, gently mix the rice from side to center. Reduce heat to low and cover with a lid.
  6. Cook the rice for an additional 20 minutes, until rice is tender and cooked through.
